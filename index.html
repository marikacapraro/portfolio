My Process

UX Research First

I start by observing how people actually use products—their workarounds, frustrations, and patterns. Understanding the problem comes before designing the solution.

Accessibility as Standard

Not a feature—a requirement. I design for WCAG compliance from the start, ensuring products work for everyone, not just the average user.

Design Meets Technology

With a solid understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I bridge design and development. I work with AI agents to rapidly prototype and validate ideas, ensuring designs are technically feasible.

From Hotel Management to UX Design

My path to UX wasn't traditional. I spent years in hospitality—managing guest experiences, anticipating needs, removing friction. Then I realized: that was UX design, just without screens.

Now I bring that same mindset to digital products. I observe real user behavior, focus on accessibility, and design solutions that feel natural. Based in Belgium, working in Italian, English, Spanish, Portuguese—and improving my French daily.
