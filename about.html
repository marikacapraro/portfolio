My story

My path to UX design started in the hospitality industry, where I spent years managing guest experiences. Every day was about anticipating needs, removing friction, and making complex systems feel effortless. I just didn't realize I was doing user experience design.

The transition clicked when I recognized that the principles are identical: understand your users, identify their pain points, design solutions that feel natural. Whether it's streamlining a hotel check-in or redesigning a booking flow, it's the same human-centered problem-solving.

After completing my UX/UI courses, I've been designing digital products with a focus on accessibility and real user needs.

I understand code—HTML, CSS, JavaScript—which helps me collaborate effectively with developers and leverage AI agents like Cursor to rapidly prototype ideas. This bridge between design and technology means I create solutions that are both user-friendly and feasible to build.
