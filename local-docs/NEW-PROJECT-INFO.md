## Key Information

### The Challenge
The City of Gent's sports booking platform served thousands of residents but had significant usability issues:
- 8-step booking process
- Hidden pricing until step 7
- Zero mobile optimization
- Poor accessibility (36 missing alt texts, low contrast)
- Confusing navigation with duplicate search patterns

### The Solution
Redesigned with mobile-first, accessibility-focused approach:
- **Reduced steps from 8 ‚Üí 5** (37.5% reduction)
- **Transparent pricing** visible from step 2
- **100% mobile responsive** with touch-friendly targets
- **WCAG AA compliant** color contrast and semantic HTML
- **Enhanced calendar** with multi-hour booking support

### Technologies Used
- **Design:** Figma (for redesign mockups)
- **Development:** React, TypeScript, Vite, Tailwind CSS, shadcn-ui
- **Features:** Multi-language support (NL/EN/FR), responsive design, accessibility
